Frequently Asked Questions

How does Focus Tree work?

Set a focus timer, put your phone down, and plant virtual trees while you study or work.

What happens if I break my focus?

If you exit the timer early, you'll lose the tree for that session.

How do I customize timer settings?

Go to Settings in the app to adjust timer lengths and notification preferences.

Is my data private?

Yes, all your focus data stays on your device. We don't collect personal information.
